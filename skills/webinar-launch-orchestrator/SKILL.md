# Webinar & Launch Orchestrator

This skill focuses on the 'Event' based sales model, creating hype and mass conversion.

## Instructions

1. Draft the pre-launch 'Teaser' campaign for social media and email.
2. Structure the webinar slide deck (Hook, Content, Pitch).
3. Plan the post-webinar 'Encore' and 'Close' sequences.
4. Manage registration flows and reminder notifications.
5. Analyze attendee engagement data to improve future events.

## Examples

- "Create a 3-week launch plan for a new software feature."
- "Outline the content for a 60-minute webinar on 'Scaling with AI Agents'."
