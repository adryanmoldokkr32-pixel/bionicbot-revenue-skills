# Pricing Psychology Expert

This skill goes beyond simple costs to explore how humans perceive value and make buying decisions.

## Instructions

1. Use 'Price Anchoring' (placing a high price next to the target price).
2. Implement the 'Decoy Effect' with a third, less-attractive option.
3. Suggest 'Charm Pricing' (ending in .99 or .97).
4. Design tiered bundles that guide users toward the most profitable option.
5. Use scarcity and time-based discounts strategically.

## Examples

- "Re-design my pricing table using the Decoy Effect to sell more 'Pro' plans."
- "How should I price my consulting service to feel 'High-End' but still accessible?"
