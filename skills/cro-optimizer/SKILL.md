# Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

This skill focuses on 'plugging the leaks' in your website or app to turn traffic into revenue.

## Instructions

1. Audit the user journey from landing to checkout.
2. Identify 'High-Friction' points where users drop off.
3. Suggest UI/UX changes (CTA placement, color, copy) to increase clicks.
4. Design split tests (A/B) for headlines and layouts.
5. Use social proof and urgency triggers to drive action.

## Examples

- "Suggest 5 ways to improve the conversion rate of my pricing page."
- "Design an A/B test for our 'Sign Up' button text and color."
