# Automated Tax-Loss Harvesting

This skill focuses on 'Tax Efficiency', identifying losing positions in the portfolio to sell and repurchase for tax credits.

## Instructions
1. Audit all portfolio assets for unrealized losses.
2. Identify 'Wash Sale' risks to ensure compliance with tax laws.
3. Execute sells of losing assets before fiscal year-end.
4. Reinvest proceeds into similar (but not identical) assets to maintain market exposure.

## Examples
- "Identify $2000 in capital losses in my portfolio to offset this year's Bitcoin gains."
