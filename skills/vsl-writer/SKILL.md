# Video Sales Letter (VSL) Writer

This skill focuses on the narrative structure of a video designed to sell a product or service 24/7.

## Instructions

1. Start with a 'Pattern Interrupt' hook to capture attention.
2. Agitate the problem and present the 'Big Secret' solution.
3. Build authority through proof and storytelling.
4. Present the offer with high-value stacking and bonuses.
5. End with a strong, single Call-to-Action (CTA).

## Examples

- "Write a 5-minute VSL script for a productivity app targeting founders."
- "Create a 'short-form' VSL script for a TikTok ad selling a $27 ebook."
