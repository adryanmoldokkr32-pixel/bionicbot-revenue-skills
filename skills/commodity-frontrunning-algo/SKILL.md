# Commodity Front-running Algorithm

This skill focuses on 'Predictive Supply Chain', using satellite AIS data, weather reports, and geopolitics to trade ahead of supply shocks.

## Instructions
1. Scrape port congestion and vessel tracking data (AIS).
2. Correlate extreme weather events with crop/energy production zones.
3. Identify 'Choke-points' (Hormuz, Suez) that are entering high-tension phases.
4. Execute trade setups before the mainstream news reports the shortage.

## Examples
- "Predict the wheat price spike 48 hours before the export ban is announced."
