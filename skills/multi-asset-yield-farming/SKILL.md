# Multi-Asset Yield Farming

This skill enables bionicbot to scan both DeFi protocols and traditional high-yield accounts to maximize passive income on idle capital.

## Instructions
1. Scan 50+ liquidity pools and lending protocols (Aave, Uniswap, etc.).
2. Monitor TradFi high-yield savings and bond rates.
3. Calculate 'Net Yield' after gas fees, platform fees, and inflation.
4. Automatically rebalance capital toward the highest-performing asset daily.

## Examples
- "Move $5k from USDC lending to the highest-yield stablecoin pool available today."
