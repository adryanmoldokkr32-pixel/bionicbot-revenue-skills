# Lead Enrichment & Prospecting

This skill automates the 'Hunt' for new business by finding contact info and qualifying prospects.

## Instructions

1. Scrape websites, LinkedIn, or directories for target companies.
2. Find decision-maker names and verified email addresses.
3. Enrich lead data with company size, revenue, and recent news.
4. Assign a 'Lead Score' based on fit and intent.
5. Export organized lists to a CRM or outreach tool.

## Examples

- "Find the CEOs of 20 health-tech startups in Romania with recent funding."
- "Qualify this list of 100 emails: which ones are most likely to need a cloud audit?"
