# E-mail Marketing Flow Builder

This skill handles the 'Long-Game' of sales through automated, relationship-building email content.

## Instructions

1. Design 'Welcome' sequences that set expectations and deliver value.
2. Create 'Abandoned Cart' or 'Browse Abandonment' reminders.
3. Implement 'Educational' sequences that lead to a product recommendation.
4. Design 'Re-engagement' flows for inactive subscribers.
5. Segment lists based on user behavior (clicked link, viewed page).

## Examples

- "Create a 7-day email sequence for new newsletter subscribers."
- "Draft a 'Win-back' email for users who haven't logged in for 30 days."
