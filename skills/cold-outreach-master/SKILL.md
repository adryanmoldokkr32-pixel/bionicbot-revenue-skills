# Cold Outreach Master

This skill focuses on reaching out to people who don't know you and getting them to say 'Yes' to a meeting.

## Instructions

1. Identify a 'Unique Insight' or 'Personalized Hook' for the prospect.
2. Write short, punchy copy that focuses on the prospect's benefit.
3. Keep the Call-to-Action (CTA) very low friction (e.g., 'Worth a chat?').
4. Set up a follow-up schedule (Day 1, 3, 7, 14).
5. Ensure deliverability by avoiding 'spammy' words and checking SPF/DKIM.

## Examples

- "Draft a cold email to a marketing head offering a free SEO audit."
- "What is the best time and channel to reach out to a busy startup founder?"
