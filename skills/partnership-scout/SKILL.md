# Sponsorship & Partnership Scout

This skill focuses on collaborative growth - getting other brands to pay you or promote you.

## Instructions

1. Analyze your audience demographics and reach.
2. Find brands whose products align with your audience's needs.
3. Create a 'Media Kit' or 'Partnership Deck' with key stats.
4. Draft outreach pitches that highlight the brand's potential ROI.
5. Negotiate deal terms (Product placement, Newsletter ads, Co-branding).

## Examples

- "Find 5 potential sponsors for a tech-focused YouTube channel."
- "Draft a pitch for a co-marketing campaign with a complementary SaaS company."
