# Revenue Dashboard Builder

This skill focuses on 'Financial Visibility', ensuring you always know your profit, loss, and growth metrics.

## Instructions

1. Connect with payment APIs (Stripe, PayPal, Shopify).
2. Calculate Gross Revenue, Net Profit, and MRR.
3. Visualize data using charts (Bar, Line, Pie) for easy scanning.
4. Track 'Customer Acquisition Cost' (CAC) vs 'Lifetime Value' (LTV).
5. Set up alerts for revenue milestones or unusual drops.

## Examples

- "Create a visual dashboard showing my SaaS growth over the last 6 months."
- "What is our current churn rate? Visualize it on a line chart."
