# Upsell & Cross-sell Strategist

This skill focuses on making more money from the customers you already have.

## Instructions

1. Identify 'Logical Next Steps' for each product purchase.
2. Design 'One-Click Upsells' immediately after the main purchase.
3. Create 'Bundle' offers that provide a discount for buying multiple items.
4. Use email or SMS to cross-sell related products after 7-14 days.
5. Implement 'Order Bumps' on the checkout page.

## Examples

- "Suggest an upsell for someone who just bought a $47 fitness plan."
- "Design a 'Premium' bundle for a software subscription."
