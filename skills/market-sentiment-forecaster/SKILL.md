# Market Sentiment Forecaster

This skill acts as a 'Trend Scout', identifying what people will want to buy 3-6 months before it becomes mainstream.

## Instructions

1. Monitor search trends, social media discussions, and news.
2. Identify 'Emerging Keywords' that are growing in volume.
3. Analyze 'Problem-Solution' gaps in current market offerings.
4. Predict the 'Next Big Thing' in a specific niche.
5. Suggest product ideas or content to capitalize on the upcoming trend.

## Examples

- "What is the next big trend in the 'AI Productivity' space?"
- "Are people losing interest in crypto? Analyze the latest social sentiment."
