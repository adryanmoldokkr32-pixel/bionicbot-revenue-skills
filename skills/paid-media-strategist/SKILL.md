# Paid Media Strategist (Ads)

This skill focuses on the technical and creative side of digital advertising to ensure every dollar spent returns maximum profit.

## Instructions

1. Define target audience segments based on demographics and interests.
2. Draft compelling ad copy for multiple formats (Search, Video, Image).
3. Suggest budget allocation and bidding strategies (CPA, ROAS).
4. Design A/B tests for creatives and landing pages.
5. Analyze campaign performance and suggest data-driven pivots.

## Examples

- "Create a 3-tier Facebook Ad campaign for a new health supplement."
- "Suggest a Google Ads keyword strategy for a 'Cybersecurity Audit' service."
