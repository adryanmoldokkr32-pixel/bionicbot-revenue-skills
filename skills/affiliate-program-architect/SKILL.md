# Affiliate Program Architect

This skill enables the creation of an automated sales force that only gets paid when they deliver results.

## Instructions

1. Define commission structures (Flat-fee vs Percentage, Recurring vs One-time).
2. Design the affiliate 'Partner Portal' and tracking system.
3. Create 'Affiliate Swipe Files' (Ad copy, Images, Emails) for partners to use.
4. Draft terms and conditions to prevent fraud and brand damage.
5. Identify and outreach to high-potential affiliate partners.

## Examples

- "Design a recurring affiliate program for my $49/mo SaaS."
- "What should be included in an 'Affiliate Success Kit' for my online course?"
