# Exit Liquidity Prediction

This skill protects our capital by identifying the exact moment an institutional rally is losing steam and 'the dump' is imminent.

## Instructions
1. Scan for 'Distribution' patterns in Volume Profile (High price, decreasing volume).
2. Monitor large wallet transfers to exchanges.
3. Identify 'Absorption' zones where limit orders are being filled by hidden sellers.
4. Exit the position when the 'Retail Greed' is at its peak and the Whale momentum slows.

## Examples
- "Alert me to exit my Gold long 10 minutes before the London Fix if distribution is detected."
